🌙 How to Read Your Answer

The stars have spoken… now what?

Not every answer is direct. Some are glowing yes’s. Some are gentle no’s. Some… are wrapped in mist. The key is to read between the sparkles. Here’s a short guide to help you interpret what your crystal ball may be telling you:

✅ Affirmative Answers (Yes, but with vibes)

  • The stars align → A beautiful yes. Everything is in sync.
  • You already know it’s yes → Deep down, you’ve always known.
  • The answer is glowing → The signs are radiant and clear.
  • Fate nods gently → Yes, but take it slow. Don’t rush fate.
  • Yes, in more ways than one → It’s a yes, and then some.

These answers are the universe smiling back at you. Sometimes direct, sometimes poetic—but they’re all gently nudging you forward.

❓ Non-Committal Answers (The fog is thick...)

  • The mist is too thick to tell → Try again later. Or look within.
  • Only time can unveil this one → Patience is part of the answer.
  • Even the stars hesitate → Not every question has one truth.
  • Ask again under a different moon → Maybe now isn’t the time.

These are not no’s. These are invitations—to pause, to reflect, or to reframe your question.

❌ Negative Answers (Soft no’s from the cosmos)

  • The crystal dims… it’s unlikely → Gently, lovingly: not this time.
  • Shadows point elsewhere → There’s another path for you.
  • The path bends away → The answer is no… for now.

No doesn't mean never. It just means: shift your gaze. There's another way.

✧ Bonus Tips ✧

  • Try rewording your question—be more specific, or more open-ended.
  • Don’t ask the same thing over and over. The stars get shy.
  • Use it for fun, reflection, or to spark conversation.

This tool isn’t about “right” or “wrong.” It’s about letting go and seeing what finds you.
