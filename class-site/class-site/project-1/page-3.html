The Sea Witch & The Bargain

The Bargain & Transformation

Determined to become human and win the prince's love, the mermaid seeks out the Sea Witch.

"I can give you legs," the witch says, "but at a price. You will lose your voice forever. Every step will feel like walking on knives. And if the prince marries another, you will dissolve into sea foam at dawn."

Despite the risks, the mermaid drinks the potion and loses consciousness.
