Love Beyond Words

The temple girl notices the mermaid’s
gentle kindness and curiosity.
Despite the mermaid’s inability to speak,
the two form a deep connection.
One day, the temple girl whispers:
“You were the one who saved him, weren’t you?”

Though the mermaid cannot confirm it,
the temple girl sees the truth in her eyes.

They run away together, leaving the palace behind.
For the first time, the mermaid feels truly seen.
